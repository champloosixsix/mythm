Scoring

Points are awarded to each driver's overall position.

First place is awarded 40 points.

Second place is awarded 38 points.

Each place underneath is awarded one less point.

Teams are allowed a max of 3 teammates and scoring is added up by best combined 3 races by the team.

You must finish 50% of the race distance to be eligible for points.

Race Format

Classe: Amateur - 2600 iRating and Below

Cars: GT3 (Audi R8 LMS, BMW M4 GT3 Prototype, Ferrari 488 Evo GT3, Ford GT GT3, Lamborghini Huracán GT3 Evo, McLaren MP4-12C GT3, Mercedes AMG GT3, Porsche 911 GT3 R)

Note: You may choose a different car for each race.

Car Settings: 50% fuel capacity, Fixed Setup (iRacing Medium Downforce Sprint)

Weather: Dynamic Weather, Track Usage at 60% (Carries Over), Marble cleaning
